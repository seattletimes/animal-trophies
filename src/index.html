Initiative I-1401
Voters this November will consider an initiative that would disallow most sales in Washington state of the following ten animals: elephants, rhinos, lions, tigers, leopards, cheetahs, pangolins, marine turtles, sharks and rays. %% of shipments through the Port of Seattle last year contained at least one of these animals.
